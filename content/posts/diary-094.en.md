Unpacking complete... for now.

Honestly, halfway through the boxes my brain went numb. Faced with pile after pile of luggage, I had no idea where to begin. I have loved sorting things since childhood: holding old objects and remembering the stories attached to them. The result, naturally, is an enormous collection of useless stuff. Growing older also infected me with the habit of hoarding merch. (It is not *that* serious...) Wait. Am I actually an anime nerd?
