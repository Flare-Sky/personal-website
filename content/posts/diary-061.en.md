Living away from home makes spending money every day almost unavoidable, and I am increasingly convinced that I need to keep a budget. On one hand, I am forever buying things on impulse; on the other, I am forever discovering things I lack. I need a daily spending limit, or it will not be long before I have to hold out my hand and ask for money again (laugh).

Reflecting on the past few days, if I were to summarize, I'd say I'm someone who is very good at setting up "flags" (resolutions) but always lacks the drive to act. For example, I still haven't finished that writeup (although the reference materials are all collected). Even though no one is rushing me, if the timeliness is gone, doesn't it lose its meaning? (But does anyone actually care about this?) Okay, writing it tomorrow!

I feel as though I have been terribly busy these last few days, yet I cannot say what kept me busy. Before the old has passed, the new has already arrived.
