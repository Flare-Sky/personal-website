Did you know? Beijing has a place called “Hangzhou Park,” and it has exactly one way in or out.

After playing basketball, guided by Geocaching, I arrived at this park. From the spot where you walk in, you can only see the sign of some hotel. Turning left at the intersection where you are prompted to turn right, this hidden little park appears right before your eyes.

Honestly, without Geocaching and Amap, I would never in a million years have walked in here. According to earlier logs, even some people who live nearby had no idea the place existed. So although today's hunt ended empty-handed—and cost me one broken camera, www—the joy of unlocking a new location still outweighed the disappointment. (MY CAMERAAAAAA.)
