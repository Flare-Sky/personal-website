My running park even contained a Premium-only cache—the kind that does not appear when I simply open the app—and I found it this morning. Its hiding place deserves special mention: directly beneath the metro tracks. The roar swelled from the distance, passed overhead, and faded away, carrying a strange sense of oppression. This was an ancient (?) cache from 2011; its container had rusted badly and the logsheet was falling apart. Next run, I need to bring a roll of tape.
